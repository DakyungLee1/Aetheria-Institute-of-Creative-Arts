Courses We Offer

Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts is dedicated to
nurturing and empowering the next generation of visionary artists, designers, and creators

Intermediate

The Foundation in Creative Exploration serves as an introductory phase where students develop a foundational understanding of various art forms and creative disciplines.

Degree

Aetheria offers degree programs in Fine Arts, Design Innovation,Performing Arts, and Creative Technologies, each emphasizing a unique blend of theory, practice, and innovation.

Post Graduation

Graduates of Aetheria go on to pursue diverse career paths in fields such as fine arts, graphic design, fashion design, animation, theater, film, digital media, advertising, and more, leveraging their creative skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the world of arts and culture
