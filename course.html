Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts is dedicated to
nurturing and empowering the next generation of visionary artists, designers, and creators

Intermediate

The Foundation in Creative Exploration serves as an introductory phase where students develop a foundational understanding of various art forms and creative disciplines.

Degree

Aetheria offers degree programs in Fine Arts, Design Innovation, Performing Arts, and Creative Technologies, each emphasizing a unique blend of theory, practice, and innovation.

Post Graduation

Graduates of Aetheria go on to pursue diverse career paths in fields such as fine arts, graphic design, fashion design, animation, theater, film, digital media, advertising, and more, leveraging their creative skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the world of arts and culture

Facilities

At the Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts, students enjoy access to
excellent facilities, fostering an inspiring environment for exploration, experimentation, and the cultivation of artistic excellence

World Class Library

The Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts boasts state-of-the-art library facilities, housing a vast collection of art books, digital resources, and creative materials to inspire and support students' artistic exploration and research endeavors

Art Studio

The Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts boasts state-of-the-art art studio facilities equipped with premium materials and tools, fostering a dynamic environment for students to unleash their creativity and bring their artistic visions to life.

Art Gallery

Aetheria Institute of Creative Arts features contemporary art gallery facilities, providing students with a platform to showcase their diverse and innovative creations to the wider community
